Our mission

Establish spiking as a
measurement standard.

Spiking is a new tool for AI governance and enables the rigorous measurement of many model properties. Project Spica will facilitate the science of spiking and establish it as a shared standard.

Use cases
01

Test set contamination

A benchmark only means something if its test data stays out of training. Insert known spikes at a known rate, then read back how strongly the model absorbed them — a calibrated estimate of how much of a held-out set has leaked, measured rather than assumed.

02

Memorization & extraction

How much of its training data does a model retain verbatim? Spikes act as controlled probes, letting researchers quantify memorization rates and the privacy and copyright risk that comes with them.

03

Unlearning verification

When data is meant to be removed, spiking offers a way to check. Track the response to known spikes before and after unlearning to measure what was actually forgotten — not just what was requested.
