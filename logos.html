Project Spica — logo concepts

The 4-point sparkle is intentionally avoided (it now reads as the generic AI / Gemini icon). In each mark the gold element is the inserted known signal — the spike — so the logo encodes the method. Ink is one flat color so every mark works in monochrome.

1 · Spīca mark

A geometric ear of wheat that doubles as an impulse spike and a measurement axis. Ties the etymology (spīca = ear of grain), Virgo's wheat ear, and the spike all at once. The crowning point is the standard candle.

4 · Wheat ear

The classical, organic Spica — Virgo's ear of wheat. Most distinctive and ownable, warmest in tone. Shown on dark to test versatility; the grain at the crown is the known candle.

Sparkline directions

The spectrum reimagined as a data trace. Each is a measurement readout where the gold is the inserted known signal. Sparklines are wide, so a square app icon would crop to the gold element.

A · Light curve

Constellation mark

The real Virgo figure from the HYG catalogue — true star positions, sized by brightness, with Spica as the gold anchor. Distinctive and deeply on-brand; the trade-off is detail, so it's best as a wordmark companion rather than a tiny favicon (a square icon would isolate Spica + a few lines).
